[ Coference Scenarios ]

The space is for critical decision-making where members can make decisions together based on presentation data; participants may be board members or in roles with decision-making authorities.​

[ Key Requirements ]

The local site requires simultaneous viewing of the presentation and remote video view, while the entire meeting needs to be recorded and audio-recorded.​

According to the topic of discussion, the contents of the screen in front of the participants can be adjusted; it can be divided into the same or different contents and switched in real time without interrupting the meeting.​

To ensure a smooth meeting, the chairman can control the meeting flow and let the participants speak in order by the microphone system.
