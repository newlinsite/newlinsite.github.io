2021

In response to the dramatic changes in the meeting and education scenarios brought about by the 2020 epidemic, we developed this software with R&D teams based on feedback from actual customers in the field to define the exact user requirements and the software specifications. The software can create a virtual USB channel to receive one or more remote video streams from your hardware devices in a secure environment, allowing customers (system integrators) to deploy a remote meeting/education environment quickly.
