Do you want to play guesstures? This site offers two games: "guesstures." and "Q&A round." This party game is developed with JavaScript, and none of the functions use any other library. These functions include a countdown function, a question bank, a custom question and background function, scoring function. This page used WEB Audio API to provide many voice interactions, including buttons and lower scores' sound effects such as soft taunts and background music that changes based on the game state.
