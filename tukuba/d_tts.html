2020 - 2021

Partner : Product manager, RD Team.

This solution is a Text-to-Speech (TTS) service that leverages advanced deep learning technology to deliver lifelike, near-native Mandarin Chinese voice synthesis with minimal effort for any speech-enable application or device.

This project attempts to build an MVP to find the market fit quickly in a traditional waterfall model development environment. After confirming the existence of the market, create a solution to meet the user needs under the assurance of R&D resources and formulate a business strategy.

What I Do

# Create a software requirements specification and maintain it with the product manager.
# Build UI flow, GUI spec, and Complete this project with two RD teams, including an RWD website and an APP.

Background

The project started with a new AI algorithm developed by our R&D team.
We need to explore its market value and create new applications.

This project begins with an innovative clone voice AI algorithm that the RD team developed, which has the features of fast speed and a small corpus (a small amount of data). Still, we don't know whether these differences can add value for the users or whether it has commercial value. This project aims to explore this AI's market value and develop corresponding applications.

We can see AI reproduce someone's voice in some videos or movies, just like the Netflix documentary series“Andy Warhol Diaries” recreated the artist's voice with AI, making us think this technique seems familiar. But creating an AI model is different, expensive, and time-consuming. In the past, it was almost a privilege for a celebrity.

Step.1 Create MVP to discover the Market

Build minimum viable products in Waterfall Model to find the market fit

The first problem with this project is that the development time is too slow to support this type of project, and at the end of the project must roll out a complete application. But we need a simple prototype to explore the market and verify whether our core functions are in need. To solve this problem, I through the following adjustment to accelerate the development.

1. Define the market which we want to explore, detailed specifications and priorities:

Define the market and function priorities clearly, making it more logical to choose or sort the function in the roadmap or PRD. In terms of market segmentation, through sales feedback, we define two target markets to test the prototype: a small business ( like YouTubers) and corporation staff ( like News reporters). And we use different sales strategies and create two applications that can meet different scenarios and are more accessible to make function trade-offs. Then Small business is through marketing promotion. Corporation through sales and BD promotion.

2. Adjust the specifications flexibly with the developed restriction

Build a fast communication workflow, more closely cooperate with the RD team, and adjust the specifications, framework, interaction, and other details with the difficulty or the restriction

3. Reduce the QA cost and schedule

Re-define which function would be needed to verify, such as how many phone models or browsers could be supported.

Through the above adjustment, the project's NRE (Non-Recurring Engineering) can be significantly reduced, and the scale of the project will be smaller. This can give PMs more space to open the next project for this solution, just like the agile model.

Step2. Based on feedback, put forward a proposal

Based on the market and sales feedback, we found some potential market demands, and we put forward design proposals for this:

Proposal 1, Translating with your voice: Use the speaker's voice to shorten the distance between the audience in a multilingual situation.

Many corporations will use interpreters to dub in international remote conferences/seminars, which are becoming more and more during the lockdown. But this often leads to feeling apart from the audience. In some situations, they need to use their voice to shorten the distance from the audience.

Proposal 2, Fast voice generation service with a large number of voice actors: Provide a secure and dedicated rental, private cloud server with multiple voice actors

With the vigorous development of podcasts, the sound economy is on the rise. Many news, magazines, story books, and other manuscripts in the market need to be converted into speech by a different voice.

Step.3 Definition

Based on the technical feasibility and channel strategy, PM selects Proposal 2 as the development direction.

We definite the function by user journey map and start design.
